About Me

My name is Karl

I am an enthusiastic, self motivated and charismatic individual.

I am from Donegal, currently living in Dublin. I am a final year student in Dublin City University studying Computer Science.
I have been interested in software and technology since a young age, which started off with gaming and eventually leading me to wonder about how the games I played were made.
I have learned many different languages and technologies both in college and outside college in my own time, and enjoy taking on personal projects in my spare time which help broaden my knowledge on topics I may not have been familiar with previously.

Aside from programming, I am an active person and enjoy going to the gym, as well as kickboxing and Jiu Jitsu.
